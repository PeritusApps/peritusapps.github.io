Support & FAQ

What's free, and what's Pro?

Free: all 330 practice questions, mastery lessons in three formats, your first full mock exam, custom flashcard decks and core progress tracking. Pro adds written explanations, every remaining mock exam, Study Habits intelligence and dark mode.

How do mastery lessons work?

Each lesson drills its questions in three formats — multiple choice, typed answer, then true/false. Anything you miss returns in a review round until you clear it (capped so sessions always end). Your lesson score counts first attempts only.

How close are mock exams to the real thing?

Mocks mirror the harmonized exam's shape: four modules sat in blocks, 75 minutes per module, with the real 60%-per-module pass rule. Rehearsal mode hides all feedback until the results screen, exactly like exam day.

Where did this Weakness Review deck come from?

When several questions from one section of a module keep going wrong, the app builds you a flashcard deck from exactly those questions. Later misses join the same deck. Delete it and it won't be rebuilt — clearing your progress lets decks be earned again.

My typed answer was right but marked wrong

Typing is graded on words, not keystrokes — small typos pass automatically. If a near-miss is judged wrong, tap "Override: I was correct" under the reveal and the app repairs your score, streak and progress on the spot.

How do I make my own flashcards?

Tap Create and you're straight in the deck builder — name the deck, write cards, delete or add rows as you go. Colour-coded borders show which cards are complete before you save.

Managing your subscription

Subscriptions are billed by Apple and managed in your device's subscription settings. Reinstalled the app or changed devices? Tap "Restore Purchases" on the upgrade screen.

Reset Progress vs Delete Account

Reset Progress wipes study history — scores, attempts, exams, streaks and auto-generated decks — but keeps your bookmarks, likes, custom decks and profile. Delete Account removes everything the app stores.

Is my data private?

Yes — everything stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. The privacy policy is one page and means it.

How do I register for the actual LLQP exam?

Through a certified course provider and your provincial regulator — the app can't register you. Once you're booked, this is where you build the speed and scenario fluency the open-book format rewards.
