Privacy Policy

Last updated July 11, 2026

LLQP Exam Prep is built by Peritus. This policy is short because our architecture is simple: your data never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no ads and no tracking of any kind. We operate no servers — the app works entirely offline, and we have no way to see how you use it.

What stays on your device

Your study progress, scores, exam history, streaks, flashcard decks, bookmarks, preferences and the optional profile name you enter are stored only in the app's private storage on your device. They are never transmitted, synced or backed up to us — we couldn't read them if we wanted to.

Purchases

Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple Account. We never see your payment details. The app checks your subscription status on-device using Apple's StoreKit framework.

Speech

Read-aloud audio is generated on-device by Apple's speech synthesis — nothing is sent anywhere.

Deleting your data

Settings → Reset Progress clears your study history. Settings → Delete Account removes everything the app stores. Deleting the app from your device has the same effect — there is no copy anywhere else.

Children

The app is a professional licensing study tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes and contact

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here and on our website with a revised date. Questions: peritusapps@gmail.com.
